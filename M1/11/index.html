1.6 There Is a Cinema, There Are Two Friends I. GRAMMAR: There is / There are

We use there is or there are to talk about

something or someone that exists in a place.

We use there is for one person or thing. There is a table in the room.

There is a doctor in the office.

We use there are for two or more people or things. There are three books on the table.

There are many students outside.
