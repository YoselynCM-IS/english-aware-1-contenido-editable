I. GRAMMAR: Verb to Be (interrogative form)

To make questions using the verb to be , we need to change the order of the words as follows:

I am

You are

He is

She is

It is

We are

You are

They are

Am I?

Are you?

Is he?

Is she?

Is it?

Are we?

Are you?

Are they?

To make short answers you use Yes or No :

Yes , I am.

Yes , you are.

Yes , he is.

Yes , she is.

Yes , it is.

Yes , we are.

Yes , you are.

Yes , they are.

No , I ’m not. No , you aren ’t. No , he isn ’t. No , she isn ’t. No , it isn ’t. No , we aren ’t. No , you aren ’t. No , they aren ’t.
