I. GRAMMAR: Articles a, an and the

We use a before a word or noun that begins in consonant sound.

a ship, a taxi, a cake, a table, a unicorn, a hotel

We use an before a word or noun that begins in vowel sound.

an alligator, an example, an ice cream, an hour, an umbrella

We use the before a specific noun or person. the teacher, the boy, the car, the book
