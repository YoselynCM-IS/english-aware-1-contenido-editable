1.7 The Phone Is on the Desk I. GRAMMAR: Prepositions of place

Prepositions of place describe where something

or someone is. They always go after some specific verbs like: be.

in                               My dog is in the house.

on                               The dog is on the house.

at                               The teacher is at the school.
