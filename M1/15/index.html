1.8 This Is My Book

I. GRAMMAR: Demonstrative

pronouns

We use demonstrative pronouns this / that / these / those to indicate where things are.

This is used to talk singular nouns that are close to us.

This is my favorite book.

That is used to talk about singular nouns that are far from us.

That is my car.

These is used to talk about two or more nouns that are near to us.

These are my keys.

Those is used to talk about two or more nouns that are far from us.

Those are my shoes.
